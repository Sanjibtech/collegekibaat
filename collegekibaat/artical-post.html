Top 10 Engineering Entrance Exams in India 2024

By Academic Expert | May 15, 2024 | 5 min read

While JEE Main and JEE Advanced are the titans of engineering entrance exams in India, relying solely on them can be risky. Diversifying your options increases your chances of securing a seat in a top-tier institution.

1. BITSAT (Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test)

BITSAT is conducted for admission to the integrated first-degree programs offered by BITS Pilani campuses in Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad. It is known for testing speed and accuracy.

2. VITEEE (VIT Engineering Entrance Examination)

Conducted by the Vellore Institute of Technology, this exam opens doors to various B.Tech programs in their Vellore, Chennai, AP, and Bhopal campuses.

3. SRMJEEE (SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination)

SRM Institute of Science and Technology conducts this exam for admission across its multiple campuses. It is usually conducted in multiple phases.
